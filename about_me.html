About Me

I am Arunkumar Govinda Bhat. I am a technical development editor at Packt Publications. I enjoy writing, reading, editing, coding and formatting.

What I do?

I help authors to write better. I edit the content and manage the entire pipeline of book editing from manuscripts to final sign off. I work on creating and editing technical documents and illustrations, to make them a better user guides.

I also help to develop ideas and set the goals and outlines for the authors to create the best technical content out there in the market. I help them to understand the keywords and set a uniform tone, style and format for the entire content.
