Me in short✌️

Hi, I am Arun. I also go by my pen name AKG Bhat.

I am a technical development editor, currently working at Packt Publications. I help authors to develop great technical content and improve their writing. I guide them to maintain a consistent tone, style and format through the technical document. I also help to maintain the pipeline of book production from manuscript stage to final editorial sign off, working along with other editors, product managers, graphics team and various external stakeholders.

I am passionate about writing, reading, editing, coding and formatting text mediums, like blogs, newsletters, books, EPUBs etc.

This is a journey to weld the mastery in the alchemy of not-so-often combinations of philosophy, language, art, science, technology, and design. I try to be creative and productive and stay simple and true to the nature of how things happen around me.
